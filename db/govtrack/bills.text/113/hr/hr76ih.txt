                                     Working Group on Jobs and Growth (HLWG) issued its
                                     final report on February 11, 2013;
                                  Whereas the economies of the European Union and the
                                     United States account for nearly one-half of world GDP;
                                  Whereas the European Union and United States account for
                                     30 percent of world trade;
                                  Whereas $2.7 billion of goods and services are traded bilat-
