HRES 2 EH

H. Res. 2

In the House of Representatives, U. S.,

January 3, 2013.

    Resolved, That the Senate be informed that a quorum of the House of Representatives has assembled; that John A. Boehner, a Representative from the State of Ohio, has been elected Speaker; and that Karen L. Haas, a citizen of the State of Maryland, has been elected Clerk of the House of Representatives of the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress.

Attest:

Clerk.
