                                    8 receipts by a roll call vote.

                                  13               ‘‘SECTION 3. Outlays for the total budget may not
                                  14 exceed the previous fiscal years’ outlays plus population

                                  17 roll call vote.
                                  18               ‘‘SECTION 4. Prior to each fiscal year, the House of

                                  22               ‘‘SECTION 5. No bill to increase revenue shall become
                                  23 law unless approved by two-thirds of the whole number




                                  24 of each House by a roll call vote.
