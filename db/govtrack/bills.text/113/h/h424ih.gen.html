HR 424 IH

113th CONGRESS

1st Session

H. R. 424

To include the county of Mohave, in the State of Arizona, as an affected area for purposes of making claims under the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act based on exposure to atmospheric nuclear testing.

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

January 25, 2013

Mr. GOSAR (for himself and Mr. FRANKS of Arizona) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary

A BILL

To include the county of Mohave, in the State of Arizona, as an affected area for purposes of making claims under the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act based on exposure to atmospheric nuclear testing.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.

    This Act may be cited as the ‘Mohave County Radiation Compensation Act of 2013’.

SEC. 2. INCLUSION UNDER THE RADIATION EXPOSURE COMPENSATION ACT.

    Section 4(b)(1)(C) of the Radiation Exposure Compensation Act (42 U.S.C. 2210 note) is amended by inserting ‘Mohave,’ after ‘Apache,’.
