                                    2              This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Mental Health in
                                    3 Schools Act of 2013’’.
                                    4     SEC. 2. PURPOSES.
                                    5              It is the purpose of this Act to—
                                    6                        (1) revise, increase funding for, and expand the
                                    7              scope of the Safe Schools-Healthy Students program
                                    8              in order to provide access to more comprehensive
                                    9              school-based mental health services and supports;
                                  10                         (2) provide for comprehensive staff development
                                  11               for school and community service personnel working
                                  12               in the school; and
                                  13                         (3) provide for comprehensive training for chil-
                                  14               dren with mental health disorders, for parents, sib-
                                  15               lings, and other family members of such children,
                                  16               and for concerned members of the community.
