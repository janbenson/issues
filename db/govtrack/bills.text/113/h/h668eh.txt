                     AN ACT
To amend section 1105(a) of title 31, United States Code,
   to require that annual budget submissions of the Presi-
   dent to Congress provide an estimate of the cost per
   taxpayer of the deficit, and for other purposes.
 1      Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representa-
 2 tives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
                               2
 1   SECTION 1. REQUIREMENT IN BUDGET SUBMISSION WITH
 2                 RESPECT TO THE COST PER TAXPAYER OF
 3                 THE DEFICIT.
 4       Section 1105(a) of title 31, United States Code, is
 5 amended—
 6            (1) redesignating paragraph (37) (relating to
 7       the list of outdated or duplicative plans and reports)
 8       as paragraph (39); and
 9            (2) by adding at the end the following:
10            ‘‘(40) in the case of a fiscal year in which the
11       budget is projected to result in a deficit, an estimate
12       of the pro rata cost of such deficit for taxpayers who
13       will file individual income tax returns for taxable
14       years ending during such fiscal year.’’.
