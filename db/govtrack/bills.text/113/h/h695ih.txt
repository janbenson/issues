                                    3     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
                                    4              This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Civilian Property Re-
                                    5 alignment Act’’ or ‘‘CPRA’’.



                                    1     SEC. 2. PURPOSES.                                  2
                                    2              The purposes of this Act are—
                                    3                        (1) to consolidate the footprint of Federal
                                    4              buildings and facilities;
                                    5                        (2) to maximize the utilization rate of Federal
                                    6              buildings and facilities;
                                    7                        (3) to reduce the reliance on leased space;
                                    8                        (4) to sell or redevelop high value assets that
                                    9              are underutilized to obtain the highest and best
                                  10               value for the taxpayer and maximize the return to
                                  11               the taxpayer;
                                  12                         (5) to reduce the operating and maintenance
                                  13               costs of Federal civilian real properties through the
                                  14               realignment of real properties by consolidating, co-
                                  15               locating, and reconfiguring space, and other oper-
                                  16               ational efficiencies;
                                  17                         (6) to reduce redundancy, overlap, and costs as-
                                  18               sociated with field offices;
                                  19                         (7) to create incentives for Federal agencies to
                                  20               achieve greater efficiency in their inventories of civil-
                                  21               ian real property;
                                  22                         (8) to facilitate and expedite the sale or dis-
                                  23               posal of unneeded civilian properties; and
                                  24                         (9) to assist Federal agencies in achieving the




                                  25               Government’s sustainability goals by reducing excess

                                    2              by leveraging new technologies.
