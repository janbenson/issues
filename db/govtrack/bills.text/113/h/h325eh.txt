    and for other purposes.

 1   SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.   2
 2       This Act may be cited as the ‘‘No Budget, No Pay
 3 Act of 2013’’.
 4   SEC. 2. TEMPORARY SUSPENSION OF DEBT CEILING.

 5       (a) SUSPENSION.—Section 3101(b) of title 31,
 6 United States Code, shall not apply for the period begin-
 7 ning on the date of the enactment of this Act and ending
 8 on May 18, 2013.
 9       (b) SPECIAL RULE RELATING           TO   OBLIGATIONS
10 ISSUED DURING SUSPENSION PERIOD.—Effective May
11 19, 2013, the limitation in section 3101(b) of title 31,
12 United States Code, as increased by section 3101A of such
13 title, is increased to the extent that—
14            (1) the face amount of obligations issued under
15       chapter 31 of such title and the face amount of obli-
16       gations whose principal and interest are guaranteed
17       by the United States Government (except guaran-
18       teed obligations held by the Secretary of the Treas-
19       ury) outstanding on May 19, 2013, exceeds
20            (2) the face amount of such obligations out-
21       standing on the date of the enactment of this Act.
22 An obligation shall not be taken into account under para-
23 graph (1) unless the issuance of such obligation was nec-
24 essary to fund a commitment incurred by the Federal Gov-
25 ernment that required payment before May 19, 2013.

3            (3)   WITHHOLDING      AND     REMITTANCE      OF
 4      AMOUNTS FROM PAYMENTS HELD IN ESCROW.—The
 5      payroll administrator shall provide for the same
 6      withholding and remittance with respect to a pay-
 7      ment deposited in an escrow account under para-
 8      graph (1) that would apply to the payment if the

10           (4) RELEASE    OF AMOUNTS AT END OF THE

22      The Secretary of the Treasury shall provide the pay-
23      roll administrators of the Houses of Congress with
