                                    1     SEC. 2. FINDINGS.                                  2
                                    2              Congress finds the following:
                                    3                        (1) Colorectal cancer is the second leading
                                    4              cause of cancer death among men and women in the
                                    5              United States, killing more non-smokers than any
                                    6              other cancer.
                                    7                        (2) Every year, more than 140,000 Americans
                                    8              are diagnosed with colorectal cancer and more than
                                    9              50,000 Americans will die from it.
                                  10                         (3) Colorectal cancer screening colonoscopy al-
                                  11               lows for the detection and removal of polyps, or ab-
                                  12               normal growths, that could become cancerous, as
                                  13               well as for the early detection of colorectal cancer
                                  14               when treatment can be most effective.
                                  15                         (4) If all precancerous polyps were identified
                                  16               and removed before becoming cancerous, estimates
                                  17               show the number of new colorectal cancer cases
                                  18               could be reduced by 76 to 90 percent and deaths
                                  19               could be reduced by 70 to 90 percent.
                                  20                         (5) Although colorectal cancer is largely pre-
                                  21               ventable, one in three adults between the ages of 50
                                  22               and 75 are not up to date with recommended
                                  23               colorectal cancer screening.
                                  24                         (6) Two-thirds of colorectal cancer cases are di-




                                  25               agnosed in patients over the age of 65.
