                                                                                             2

                                  13                         (1) by striking ‘‘(7) All’’ and inserting ‘‘(7)(A)
                                  14               Except as provided in subparagraph (B), all’’; and
                                  15                         (2) by adding at the end the following:
                                  16                         ‘‘(B) No real property, including any right,
                                  17               title, and interest in the whole of any lot or tract of
                                  18               land and any appurtenances or improvements, shall
                                  19               be subject to forfeiture under subparagraph (A) due
                                  20               to medical marijuana-related conduct that is author-
                                  21               ized by State law.’’.
