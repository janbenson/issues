15       ‘‘(a) PROGRAM.—The Secretary shall establish a pro-
16 gram consisting of awarding demonstration grants to
17 States to streamline State requirements and procedures
18 in order to assist veterans who completed military emer-
19 gency medical technician training while serving in the
20 Armed Forces of the United States to meet certification,
21 licensure, and other requirements applicable to becoming
22 an emergency medical technician in the State.
23       ‘‘(b) USE   OF   FUNDS.—Amounts received as a dem-
24 onstration grant under this section shall be used to pre-
25 pare and implement a plan to streamline State require-
