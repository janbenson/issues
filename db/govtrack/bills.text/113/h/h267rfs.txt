                                  13                         (3) as of the date of enactment of this Act, hy-
                                  14               dropower resources, including pumped storage facili-
                                  15               ties, provide—
                                  16                                  (A) nearly 7 percent of the electricity gen-
                                  17                         erated in the United States; and
                                  18                                  (B) approximately 100,000 megawatts of
                                  19                         electric capacity in the United States;
                                  20
                                                             (4) only 3 percent of the 80,000 dams in the
                                  21               United States generate electricity, so there is sub-
