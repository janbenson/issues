HR 350 IH

113th CONGRESS

1st Session

H. R. 350

To repeal the Legal Services Corporation Act.

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

January 22, 2013

Mr. AUSTIN SCOTT of Georgia (for himself, Mr. WESTMORELAND, Mr. DUNCAN of South Carolina, and Mr. MULVANEY) introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on the Judiciary

A BILL

To repeal the Legal Services Corporation Act.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That the Legal Services Corporation Act is repealed.
