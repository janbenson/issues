                                    4     SEC. 2. FINDINGS.

                                    5                        (1) Since September 11, 2001, approximately
                                    6              30,000 veterans have been separated from the
                                    7              Armed Forces on the basis of a personality disorder
                                    8              or adjustment disorder.
                                    9                        (2) Nearly all veterans who are separated on
                                  10               the basis of a personality or adjustment disorder are
                                  11               prohibited from accessing service-connected disability
                                  12               compensation, disability severance pay, and disability
                                  13               retirement pay.
                                  14                         (3) Many veterans who are separated on the
                                  15               basis of a personality or adjustment disorder are un-
                                  16               able to find employment because of the ‘‘personality
                                  17               disorder’’ or ‘‘adjustment disorder’’ label on their
                                  18               Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active
                                  19               Duty.
                                  20                         (4) The Government Accountability Office has
                                  21               found that the regulatory compliance of the Depart-
                                  22               ment of Defense in separating members of the
                                  23               Armed Forces on the basis of a personality or ad-
                                  24               justment disorder was as low as 40 percent between




                                  25               2001 and 2007.
