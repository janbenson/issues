                                    6              quintupled over the past decade from 400 to more
                                    7              than 2,000.

                                    9              centers of the House of Representatives, has reduced
                                  10               electricity expenses and requisite real estate where it
                                  11               has been pursued.

                                  13               annually, while most existing Federal facilities do
                                  14               not meet efficiency standards that are expected for




                                  15               new Federal construction.
