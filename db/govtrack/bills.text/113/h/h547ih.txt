                                    5              The purposes of this Act are to provide a means
                                    6 whereby Federal lands and resources along the United
                                    7 States-Mexico border are provided the highest protection
                                    8 possible from the effects of unauthorized immigration,
                                    9 human and drug smuggling, and border enforcement ac-

                                  11 achieve border security are undertaken.
