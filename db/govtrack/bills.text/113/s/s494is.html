To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to expand the types of livestock for which bonus depreciation is available.

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

Mr. SCHUMER introduced the following bill; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance

A BILL

To amend the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 to expand the types of livestock for which bonus depreciation is available.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. EXPANSION OF LIVESTOCK AVAILABLE FOR BONUS DEPRECIATION.

    (a) In General- Subparagraph (E) of section 168(k)(2) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 is amended by adding at the end the following new clause:

          ‘(v) DAIRY PRODUCING CALVES AND COWS- In the case of any dairy producing calf or cow, subparagraph (A) shall be applied without regard to clause (ii) thereof.’.

    (b) Effective Date- The amendment made by subsection (a) shall apply to property placed in service after December 31, 2012, in taxable years ending after such date.
