113TH CONGRESS
   1ST SESSION      S. CON. RES. 1
     CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
 1       Resolved by the Senate (the House of Representatives
 2 concurring), That provide the two Houses of Congress
 3 shall meet in the Hall of the House of Representatives
 4 on Thursday, the 4th day of January 2013, at 1 o’clock
 5 post meridian, pursuant to the requirements of the Con-
 6 stitution and laws relating to the election of President and
 7 Vice President of the United States, and the President
 8 of the Senate shall be their Presiding Officer; that two
 9 tellers shall be previously appointed by the President of
10 the Senate on the part of the Senate and two by the
11 Speaker on the part of the House of Representatives, to
12 whom shall be handed, as they are opened by the Presi-
13 dent of the Senate, all the certificates and papers pur-
14 porting to be certificates of the electoral votes, which cer-
15 tificates and papers shall be opened, presented, and acted
16 upon in the alphabetical order of the States, beginning
17 with the letter ‘‘A’’; and said tellers, having then read the
18 same in the presence and hearing of the two Houses, shall
19 make a list of the votes as they shall appear from said
                            2
1 certificates; and the votes having been ascertained and
2 counted in the manner and according to the rules by law
3 provided, the result of the same shall be delivered to the
4 President of the Senate, who shall thereupon announce the
5 state of the vote, which announcement shall be deemed
6 a sufficient declaration of the persons, if any, elected
7 President and Vice President of the United States, and,
8 together with a list of the votes, be entered on the Jour-
9 nals of the two Houses.
       Passed the Senate January 3, 2013.
       Attest:




                                               Secretary.
