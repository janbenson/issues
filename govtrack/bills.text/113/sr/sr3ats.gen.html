SRES 3 ATS

113th CONGRESS

1st Session

S. RES. 3

Fixing the hour of daily meeting of the Senate.

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

January 3, 2013

Mr. REID (for himself and Mr. MCCONNELL) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to

RESOLUTION

Fixing the hour of daily meeting of the Senate.

    Resolved, That the daily meeting of the Senate be 12 o’clock meridian unless otherwise ordered.
