SRES 20 ATS

113th CONGRESS

1st Session

S. RES. 20

Designating Chairman of the Senate Committee on Foreign Relations.

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

January 29, 2013

Mr. REID submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to

RESOLUTION

Designating Chairman of the Senate Committee on Foreign Relations.

    Resolved, The Senator from New Jersey, Mr. Menendez, shall be the Chairman of the Committee on Foreign Relations for the One Hundred Thirteenth Congress, or until his successor is chosen.

    Sec. 2. Provided, That this resolution shall be effective upon the resignation of the Senator from Massachusetts (Mr. Kerry).
