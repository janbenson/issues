SRES 2 ATS

113th CONGRESS

1st Session

S. RES. 2

Informing the House of Representatives that a quorum of the Senate is assembled.

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

January 3, 2013

Mr. REID (for himself and Mr. MCCONNELL) submitted the following resolution; which was considered and agreed to

RESOLUTION

Informing the House of Representatives that a quorum of the Senate is assembled.

    Resolved, That the Secretary inform the House of Representatives that a quorum of the Senate is assembled and that the Senate is ready to proceed to business.
