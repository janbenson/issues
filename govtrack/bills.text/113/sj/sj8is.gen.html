S. J. RES. 8

Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Internal Revenue Service of the Department of the Treasury relating to taxable medical devices.

IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES

February 27, 2013

Mr. PAUL introduced the following joint resolution; which was read twice and referred to the Committee on Finance

JOINT RESOLUTION

Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the Internal Revenue Service of the Department of the Treasury relating to taxable medical devices.

    Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled, That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the Internal Revenue Service of the Department of the Treasury relating to taxable medical devices (published at 77 Fed. Reg. 72924 (December 7, 2012)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
