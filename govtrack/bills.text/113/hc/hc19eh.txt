   1ST SESSION     H. CON. RES. 19
    CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
1      Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate
2 concurring),
3   SECTION 1. USE OF CAPITOL GROUNDS FOR SOAP BOX
4                DERBY RACES.
5      (a) IN GENERAL.—The Greater Washington Soap
6 Box Derby Association (in this resolution referred to as
7 the ‘‘sponsor’’) shall be permitted to sponsor a public
                                   2
 1 event, soap box derby races (in this resolution referred to
 2 as the ‘‘event’’), on the Capitol Grounds.
 3       (b) DATE      OF   EVENT.—The event shall be held on
 4 June 15, 2013, or on such other date as the Speaker of
 5 the House of Representatives and the Committee on Rules
 6 and Administration of the Senate jointly designate.
 7   SEC. 2. TERMS AND CONDITIONS.

 8       (a) IN GENERAL.—Under conditions to be prescribed
 9 by the Architect of the Capitol and the Capitol Police
10 Board, the event shall be—
11            (1) free of admission charge and open to the
12       public; and
13            (2) arranged not to interfere with the needs of
14       Congress.
15       (b) EXPENSES       AND   LIABILITIES.—The sponsor shall
16 assume full responsibility for all expenses and liabilities
17 incident to all activities associated with the event.
18   SEC. 3. EVENT PREPARATIONS.
19       Subject to the approval of the Architect of the Cap-
20 itol, the sponsor is authorized to erect upon the Capitol
21 Grounds such stage, sound amplification devices, and
22 other related structures and equipment as may be required
23 for the event.




      •HCON 19 EH          3
 1   SEC. 4. ADDITIONAL ARRANGEMENTS.
 2       The Architect of the Capitol and the Capitol Police
 3 Board are authorized to make such additional arrange-
 4 ments as may be required to carry out the event.
 5   SEC. 5. ENFORCEMENT OF RESTRICTIONS.
 6       The Capitol Police Board shall provide for enforce-
 7 ment of the restrictions contained in section 5104(c) of
 8 title 40, United States Code, concerning sales, advertise-
 9 ments, displays, and solicitations on the Capitol Grounds,
10 as well as other restrictions applicable to the Capitol
11 Grounds, with respect to the event.
