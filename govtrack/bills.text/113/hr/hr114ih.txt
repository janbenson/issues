                                                              RESOLUTION
                                              Recognizing the Sabin Vaccine Institute on the 20th
                                                          anniversary of its founding.

                                  Whereas the Sabin Vaccine Institute was founded in honor of
                                     the late Dr. Albert B. Sabin, a distinguished scientific
                                     figure in the history of medicine and the developer of the
                                     oral live-virus polio vaccine, who died on March 3, 1993;
                                  Whereas for the past 20 years, the Sabin Vaccine Institute
                                     has demonstrated noteworthy success in its mission to re-





                                     duce the prevalence of neglected tropical diseases (NTDs)
                                     by developing new vaccines, advocating for increased use

                                     collaborators worldwide, is dedicated to the creation of
                                     vaccines to prevent NTDs;

                                     controlling and eliminating the seven most common

                                     phatic filariais (elephantiasis), schistosomiasis (snail

                                  Whereas the Sabin Vaccine Institute attempts to break the
