                                    6     SEC. 2. FINDINGS.
                                    7              Congress finds the following:




                                                                                             2
                                    1                        (1) Through careful development, teachers can
                                    2              build their effectiveness over time, thus improving
                                    3              student achievement.
                                    4                        (2) Ongoing professional development of teach-
                                    5              ers in the subjects they teach is essential for im-
                                    6              proved student learning.
                                    7                        (3) United States teachers generally spend
                                    8              more time instructing students and less time in pro-
                                    9              fessional learning opportunities with their peers than
                                  10               teachers in top-performing countries.
                                  11                         (4) It takes time, resources, and support for
                                  12               teachers to become highly effective in their class-
                                  13               rooms. Teachers participating in quality teacher pro-
                                  14               fessional development must be given time to imple-
                                  15               ment what they have learned.
