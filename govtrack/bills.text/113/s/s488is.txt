                                    3                        (1) to reform and reorient the vehicle tech-

                                    5                        (2) to establish a clear and consistent authority

                                    8              practices that—

                                  11                         States; and
                                  12                                  (B) reduce vehicle reliance on petroleum-

                                  14                         (4) to support domestic research, development,

                                  16               tion and manufacturing of advanced vehicles, en-
                                  17               gines, and components;

                                    5              nologies for wide-scale utilization throughout the
                                    6              passenger, commercial, government, and transit ve-
