                                                                                                                  A BILL
                                                                        To implement equal protection under the 14th article of
                                                                           amendment to the Constitution for the right to life of
                                                                           each born and preborn human person.
                                                                          1              Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representa-
                                                                          2 tives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
                                                                          3     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
                                                                          4              This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Life at Conception Act
                                                                          5 of 2013’’.


                                                                          1     SEC. 2. RIGHT TO LIFE.                             2
                                                                          2              To implement equal protection for the right to life
                                                                          3 of each born and preborn human person, and pursuant
                                                                          4 to the duty and authority of the Congress, including Con-
                                                                          5 gress’ power under article I, section 8, to make necessary
                                                                          6 and proper laws, and Congress’ power under section 5 of
                                                                          7 the 14th article of amendment to the Constitution of the
                                                                          8 United States, the Congress hereby declares that the right
                                                                          9 to life guaranteed by the Constitution is vested in each
                                                                        10 human being. Nothing in this Act shall be construed to
                                                                        11 require the prosecution of any woman for the death of her
                                                                        12 unborn child.
                                                                        13      SEC. 3. DEFINITIONS.
                                                                        14               In this Act:
                                                                        15                         (1) HUMAN                 PERSON;              HUMAN            BEING.—The
                                                                        16               terms ‘‘human person’’ and ‘‘human being’’ include
                                                                        17               each member of the species homo sapiens at all
                                                                        18               stages of life, including the moment of fertilization,
                                                                        19               cloning, or other moment at which an individual
                                                                        20               member of the human species comes into being.
                                                                        21                         (2) STATE.—The term ‘‘State’’, and as used in
                                                                        22               the 14th article of amendment to the Constitution of
                                                                        23               the United States and other applicable provisions of
                                                                        24               the Constitution, includes the District of Columbia,
                                      



                                                                        25               the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, and each other
                                                                        26               territory or possession of the United States.
