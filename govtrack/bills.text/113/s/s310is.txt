                                    2              Congress makes the following findings:
                                    3                        (1) Achieving economic recovery will require the
                                    4              formation and growth of new companies.
                                    5                        (2) Between 1980 and 2005, companies less
                                    6              than 5 years old accounted for nearly all net job cre-
                                    7              ation in the United States.
                                    8                        (3) New firms in the United States create an
                                    9              average of 3,000,000 jobs per year.
                                  10                         (4) To get Americans back to work, entre-
                                  11               preneurs must be free to innovate, create new com-
                                  12               panies, and hire employees.
