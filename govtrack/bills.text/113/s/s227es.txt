 1   SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.      2
 2       This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Embassy Security
 3 Funds Transfer Act of 2013’’.
 4   SEC. 2. TRANSFER OF CERTAIN FUNDS FOR IMPROVEMENT
 5                    OF SECURITY AT UNITED STATES EMBASSIES
 6                    AND OTHER DIPLOMATIC FACILITIES WORLD-
 7                    WIDE.
 8       (a) TRANSFER AUTHORITY.—Funds appropriated by
 9 title VIII of the Department of State, Foreign Operations,
10 and Related Programs Appropriations Act of 2012 (divi-
11 sion I of Public Law 112–74; 125 Stat. 1265) under the
12 headings ‘‘DIPLOMATIC         AND   CONSULAR PROGRAMS’’ and
13 ‘‘EMBASSY SECURITY, CONSTRUCTION,              AND   MAINTE-
14   NANCE’’   may be transferred between such headings.
15       (b) AVAILABILITY.—
16                 (1) IN   GENERAL.—Any   funds transferred to a
17       heading under subsection (a) shall be merged with
18       funds in the heading to which transferred, and shall,
19       except as provided in paragraph (2), be available
20       subject to the same terms and conditions as the
21       funds with which merged.
22                 (2) DURATION   OF AVAILABILITY.—Any     funds
23       transferred under subsection (a) shall be available
24       for the same period for which such funds were origi-
25       nally appropriated.
