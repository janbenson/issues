                                  To amend the Immigration and Nationality Act to eliminate discrimination
                                      in the immigration laws by permitting permanent partners of United
                                      States citizens and lawful permanent residents to obtain lawful perma-
                                      nent resident status in the same manner as spouses of citizens and
                                      lawful permanent residents and to penalize immigration fraud in connec-
                                      tion with permanent partnerships.
