                                                                            A BILL
                                        To amend chapter 2 of title I of the United States Code
                                              to establish the style for amending laws.
                                    1              Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representa-
                                    2 tives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
                                    3     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE.
                                    4              This Act may be cited as the ‘‘Readable Legislation
                                    5 Act of 2013’’.
                                    6     SEC. 2. AMENDING LAWS.
                                    7              Chapter 2 of title 1 of the United States Code is
                                    8 amended by adding at the end the following new section:





                                                                                             2
                                    1 ‘‘§ 115. Amending laws
                                    2              ‘‘No Act shall be revised or amended by mere ref-
                                    3 erence to it. Every bill or joint resolution which amends
                                    4 an existing section, subsection, or other subdivision of any
                                    5 Act shall set forth the section, subsection, or other subdivi-
                                    6 sion sufficiently to enable the intent and effect of the bill
                                    7 or joint resolution to be clearly understood. Omissions and
                                    8 insertions proposed shall be shown by appropriate typo-
                                    9 graphical devices.’’.
                                  10      SEC. 3. CONFORMING AMENDMENT.
                                  11               The table of sections at the beginning of chapter 2
                                  12 of title 1 of the United States Code is amended by adding
                                  13 after the item relating to section 114 the following new
                                  14 item:‘‘115. Amending laws.’’.
                                  15      SEC. 4. EFFECTIVE DATE.
                                  16               This Act and the amendments made by it shall take
                                  17 effect on January 3, 2015.
