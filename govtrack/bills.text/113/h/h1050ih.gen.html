H. R. 1050

To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Disperse Red 73.

IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

March 11, 2013

Mr. WATT introduced the following bill; which was referred to the Committee on Ways and Means

A BILL

To extend the temporary suspension of duty on Disperse Red 73.

    Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,

SECTION 1. DISPERSE RED 73.

    (a) In General- Heading 9902.03.57 of the Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (relating to Disperse Red 73) is amended by striking the date in the effective period column and inserting ‘12/31/2016’.

    (b) Effective Date- The amendment made by subsection (a) applies to goods entered, or withdrawn from warehouse for consumption, on or after the 15th day after the date of the enactment of this Act.
