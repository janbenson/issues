                                                                            A BILL
                                  To prohibit the National Labor Relations Board from taking
                                     any action that requires a quorum of the members of
                                     the Board until such time as Board constituting a
                                     quorum shall have been confirmed by the Senate, the
                                     Supreme Court issues a decision on the constitutionality
                                     of the appointments to the Board made in January 2012,
                                     or the adjournment sine die of the first session of the
                                     113th Congress.

                                    4     SEC. 2. ACTIVITIES BY THE NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS
                                    5                             BOARD PROHIBITED.
                                    6              Effective on the date of enactment of this Act, the
                                    7 National Labor Relations Board shall cease all activity
                                    8 that requires a quorum of the members of the Board, as
                                    9 set forth in the National Labor Relations Act (29 U.S.C.
                                  10 151 et seq.). The Board shall not implement, administer,

                                  12 undertaken, adopted, issued, or finalized on or after Janu-
                                  13 ary 4, 2012, that requires a quorum of the members of
                                  14 the Board, as set forth in such Act.
