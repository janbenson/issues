                                                                            A BILL
                                  To prevent certain individuals purportedly appointed to the
                                     National Labor Relations Board from receiving salaries,
                                     and to prevent an unconstitutional quorum of the Board
                                     from taking agency actions, until there is a final decision
                                     in pending lawsuits regarding the constitutionality of
                                     certain alleged recess appointments.
