                                    1     SEC. 2. FINDINGS.                                  2
                                    2              Congress finds the following:
                                    3                        (1) The inland waterways navigation system is
                                    4              vital to the economic well-being of the Nation.
                                    5                        (2) Energy reliability and conservation are the
                                    6              cornerstones of the inland waterways navigation sys-
                                    7              tem.
                                    8                        (3) The efficiency of the inland waterways navi-
                                    9              gation system creates the optimum transportation
                                  10               mode for bulk commodities in the United States.
                                  11                         (4) The inland waterways navigation system
                                  12               provides for environmental protection and ecosystem
                                  13               sustainability.
                                  14      SEC. 3. PURPOSES.
                                  15               In order to ensure continued safe, dependable, highly
                                  16 cost-effective, and environmentally sustainable navigation
                                  17 on the inland and intracoastal waterways of the United
                                  18 States, the purposes of this Act are to—
                                  19                         (1) improve program and project management
                                  20               applicable to the construction and major rehabilita-
                                  21               tion of navigation projects on such waterways;
                                  22                         (2) optimize inland waterways navigation sys-
                                  23               tem reliability;
                                  24                         (3) minimize the size and scope of inland water-




                                  25               ways navigation project completion schedules;

                                    3                        (5) make inland waterways navigation capital
                                    4              investments through use of prioritization criteria
                                    5              that seek to maximize system-wide benefits and min-
                                    6              imize overall system risk.
