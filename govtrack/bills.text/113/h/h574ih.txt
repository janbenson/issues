                                  To amend part B of title XVIII of the Social Security

                                    1     SECTION 1. SHORT TITLE; PURPOSE.                   2
                                    2              (a) SHORT TITLE.—This Act may be cited as the
                                    3 ‘‘Medicare Physician Payment Innovation Act of 2013’’.
                                    4              (b) PURPOSE.—The purpose of this Act is to reform
                                    5 the system of Medicare payment for physicians’ services—
                                    6                        (1) by ending the application of the sustainable
                                    7              growth rate (SGR) system;
                                    8                        (2) to stabilize payments for 2014;
                                    9                        (3) to promote the rapid development and im-
                                  10               plementation of alternative improved payment and
                                  11               delivery models that incentivize high quality, high-
                                  12               value care; and
                                  13                         (4) to provide continuing incentives for adop-
                                  14               tion of such alternative payment and delivery models
                                  15               by physicians and other providers.
